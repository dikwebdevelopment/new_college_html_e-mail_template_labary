Html-E-Mail Resource Library

To use these templates simply find one you want to use. Then click and drag over the template and paste into your e-mail composer. Then Click On the section of text you would like to change and make your updates. The changes made will take on the style set to the section. The links in the form have pre set destinations but can be deleted. When all Changes to the template are Complete hit send.

Copy And Paste Template Between Lines Into Text Editor Or Email Composer of your choice To Edit & Send. Do not copy outside these lines
